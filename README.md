# Nova ¡ª AI Knowledge Studio

A local-first knowledge management app with AI-powered insights, bidirectional linking, and a beautiful knowledge graph.

## Features

- **Markdown Editor** with live preview, split view, and distraction-free writing
- **Bidirectional Links** using [[wikilinks]] to connect your ideas
- **Knowledge Graph** physics-based visualization of your note connections
- **Smart Tagging** with tags for organization and discovery
- **AI-Powered Search** with relevance ranking and tag filtering
- **Local AI Engine** summarization, keyword extraction, tone analysis, and related notes ¡ª all in your browser
- **Dark/Light Theme** beautiful design system with smooth transitions
- **Local-First** your data stays on your machine in IndexedDB
- **Export** notes to JSON for backup or migration

## Quick Start

1. Open 'nova/index.html' in your browser (Chrome or Firefox recommended)
2. Create your first note and start writing
3. Use links to connect ideas and tags to organize
4. Explore the Graph tab to see your knowledge network
5. Check the Pricing at 'nova/landing/index.html' for license options
